How it works

Three clicks. Clean output.

Data Bouncer lives in Chrome's side panel and only runs when you ask it to. Here's what it does — and what the result looks like.

Step 01
🧩

Open the side panel

Click the Data Bouncer icon in your toolbar. The panel opens alongside the page — no new tab, no popup.

Step 02
⚡

Hit Convert

One button. Readability extracts the content, Turndown converts it to Markdown, then PII detection runs on the result.

Step 03
📋

Copy and paste safely

Sensitive values become readable placeholders like {{EMAIL_1}}. You get the content. Not the liability.
